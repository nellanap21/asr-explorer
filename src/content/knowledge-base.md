# Competitive Knowledge Base

## Gong

<!--
id: gong
type: competitor
keywords: gong, gong.io, revenue intelligence, call recording
-->

### Differentiators

- Provides assistance during the live conversation.
- Surfaces approved objection handling while the seller is speaking.
- Focuses on real-time seller guidance rather than only post-call analysis.

### Suggested response

Ask whether the buyer is looking for post-call analytics or live assistance.

---

## Salesforce

<!--
id: salesforce
type: competitor
keywords: salesforce, sales cloud, einstein
-->

### Differentiators

- Works alongside an existing CRM rather than replacing it.
- Focuses specifically on live conversation guidance.
- Can surface relevant internal sales knowledge during the call.

### Suggested response

Clarify whether the buyer is comparing the product to a CRM or to a sales-assistance platform.

---

## Pricing objection

<!--
id: pricing-objection
type: objection
keywords: expensive, pricing, price, budget, cost, too much
-->

### Guidance

- Ask what budget range the buyer had expected.
- Clarify whether the concern is total price or expected return on investment.
- Avoid immediately offering a discount.
